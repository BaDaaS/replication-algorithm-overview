# Module 0039 Exercises

## Exercise 1 [T]: Atlas quorum intersection

Verify that Atlas's fast and slow quorums intersect for any
admissible `(n, f, c)`.

## Exercise 2 [P]: tuning c

For a workload with measured 5% conflict rate, recommend a
value of c. Discuss the trade-off.

## Exercise 3 [F]: pseudo-Lean Atlas safety

State Atlas's safety theorem in pseudo-Lean. Identify the
quorum-intersection lemma.

## Exercise 4 [V]: verifiable Atlas

Estimate the per-command SNARK constraint cost for verifiable
Atlas. Compare to EPaxos.
